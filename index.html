About SFIT

St. Francis Institute of Technology (SFIT) was established in 1999 with a view to create an Engineering Degree Institute to fulfill the long felt public need of an Institute of Excellence in Borivali. SFIT has five UG Programs and two PG Programs in the most coveted disciplines considering both industrial need and public preference. These courses would lead to award of the Degree of Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) in Computer Engineering, Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering, Information Technology, Electrical Engineering and Mechanical Engineering and Master of Engineering (M.E.) as well as Ph.D. (Technology) in Computer Engineering and Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering.
